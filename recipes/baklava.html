Mouth Watering Baklava

Baklava is a desert to be experienced. It has an amazing combination of textures that is sure to please. The paper-thin layers of filo pastry are flaky and crisp while the nut filling is sweet and chewy. Addictive.

Baklava

Ingredients:

  • 1 recipe Filo (Phyllo) Dough
  • 1 recipe syrup
  • 1 recipe nut filling
  • 1 recipe browned butter

Syrup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (200 grams) white granulated sugar
  • ½ cup (118 milliliters) water
  • 1 teaspoon (5 milliliters) lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon (2.5 milliliters) orange blossom water

Steps:

  1. Butter a 9½ inch (24 cm) pan with browned butter.
  2. Place one sheets of filo pastry in the pan and butter thoroughly. Repeat until you have 5 sheets layered.
  3. Sprinkle ½ cup of the nut mixture on top. Top with another 5 sheets of filo. Butter each layer with brown butter. Repeat as follows: 5 filo —> nuts —> 5 filo —> nuts —> 5 filo —> nuts —> 5 filo.
  4. Cut into the desired shape.
    • Start off with a round cut

  5. Pour 3 tablespoon browned butter
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 300°F/149°C for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Pour the cold syrup on the hot baklava and let it cool down completely before serving
